Hi! I'm **Liuchao Jin (金流超)**, a **[Hong Kong PhD Fellow](https://cerg1.ugc.edu.hk/hkpfs/index.html)** and Ph.D. candidate in Mechanical & Automation Engineering at [The Chinese University of Hong Kong](https://www.cuhk.edu.hk/english/index.html), advised by Prof. [Wei-Hsin Liao](https://www4.mae.cuhk.edu.hk/peoples/liao-wei-hsin/). I develop **machine-learning-driven design methods for 3D/4D printing, smart materials & structures, metamaterials, and soft robotics** — with applications in energy absorption and energy harvesting.

I also serve as a visiting scholar at the [Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech)](https://www.sustech.edu.cn/en/) and [Shenzhen University (SZU)](https://en.szu.edu.cn/). My academic journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from [Sichuan University - Pittsburgh Institute (SCUPI)](https://scupi.scu.edu.cn/en/), where I graduated ranked 1st in my program.
